about me // về tôi

As a freshly enrolled UX university student at THUAS with a hunger to learn, I am eager to delve into the field of UX design and to explore methods of utilizing technology to create intuitive and user-focused experiences. My attention to details and my intuition for design are some of my greatest asset which helped me kick start my career.

I am looking to use my skills to create innovative and meaningful digital experiences. I am particularly interested in working on projects in the areas of UX design, interaction design, user research and product design. My passion for understanding user needs, exploring user behavior and creating intuitive designs will enable me to create meaningful digital experiences.

My design style is characterized by a focus on function and usability, with a touch boldness and contemporary. I believe that good design should enable users to accomplish their tasks quickly and effectively, no matter their background or level of technical expertise. To ensure a great user experience, I always strive to keep things simple and navigate quickly. On a personal level, I'm a hardworking, creative and detail-oriented individual. I thrive when I am part of a collaborative environment with plenty of back and forth, inspiring discussions and creative freedom. I love working on innovative projects, and I'm always looking for ways to improve my craft.
